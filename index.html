About

SHREE LAXMI PRASAD DEVKOTA SECONDARY SCHOOL-LPDSS is the Public school which provide both Nepali & English medium Educationto Students. It is located in Kachankawal-02 sukunadigi, jhapa Nepal. It was Established in 2026. About 1,000 Students are studying here. We are Providng Quality Education. We are working better to provide well maintain Education here. Our school is best Public school of Kachankawal rural municipality. We provide Education from Nursery class to 12 class. As technology increasing , we have enough Computer to provide computer training. We have 2 Computer Lap for the students. Students can read computer From 4 to 8 class & in +2 level.
